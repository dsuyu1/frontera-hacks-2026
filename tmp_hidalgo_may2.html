May 2, 2026 Local Entities Elections

Welcome to the May 2, 2026 Local Entities Election page. 

Please note that this page will be updated with information as soon as it becomes available.

STAY IN THE KNOW, by visiting our website in the next couple of weeks. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact our office at (956)318-2570.

Early voting will begin Monday, April 20, 2026 and will run to Tuesday, April 28, 2026. With the exception of Tuesday, April 21, 2026 and Sunday, April 26, 2026 polling locations will be closed. Election Day is Saturday, May 2, 2026

Election Calendar Dates

First Day to Apply for a Ballot by Mail Thursday, January 1, 2026
Last Day to Register to Vote Thursday, April 2, 2026

Last Day to Apply for Ballot by Mail (Received, not Postmarked)Monday, April 20, 2026
Last Day of Early VotingTuesday, April 28, 2026
Election Day Saturday, May 2, 2026
